Mistake #2, eating before practice.

The itis has come to claim me victim to the lures of sleep. Although I'm highly enjoying my piano practice time, I found myself slumping over in my seat due to the effects of the food I just ate. After a brief nap, I woke up and am now cranking through this posts. I think I'll bullet point most of my learned topics for today since nothing was (too) in depth.
On Day B of my cycle I covered the following:

  • Running
  • Piano
  • Medium Programming time
  • Light Japanese

As far as programming goes, I'm still grinding away at the background knowdledge needed for signal processing and its subset, Onset Detection. I was actually able to find a nice article showing how to do low-pass filters in Python which may prove useful to me understanding the field as a whole.

In regards to piano, I am making slow but steady progress with the fundamentals. I have learned various things such as how to actually sit at the piano, the labelling of the keys as well as the proper way to finger keys. To anyone experienced in piano, this will probably seems like a baby's first steps in regards to the process but I dont mind. Progress, even slow progress every day is good.

Now off to sleep...I must learn from this mistake otherwise my lessons will keep getting cut short.

--RaShaun
